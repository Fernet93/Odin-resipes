French Onion Soup

Rich and Simple French Onion Soup

We have been trying French onion soup in restaurants for years and my family and friends agree none can compare to my recipe for taste and simplicity of preparation.

Ingredients

  • Half a cup butter
  • 2 tbls olive oil
  • 4 Cups sliced unions
  • 5 cups beef broth
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 4 slices of French bread
  • 4 slices of provolone cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter with olive oil in an 8-quart stock pot over medium heat. Add onions and continually stir until tender and translucent. Do not brown the onions.
  2. Add beef broth, sherry, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper. Let simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Ladle soup into oven-safe serving bowls and place one slice of bread on top of each (bread may be broken into pieces if you prefer). Layer each slice of bread with a slice of provolone, 1/2 slice diced Swiss and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese.
  4. Place bowls on a cookie sheet and broil in the preheated oven until cheese bubbles and browns slightly, 2 to 3 minutes.
