Chicken fried rice

Ingredients

For this simple and delicious dish you will only need a few Ingredients

  1. Rice
  2. Chicken
  3. Eggs
  4. White or red onion
  5. Carrots
  6. Peas
  7. Spring onions

Steps

  • First boil the rice or use yesterdays
  • Then fry up the chicken and remove from the pan
  • Whisk the eggs and make scrambled eggs and take off the pan
  • Add all the vegetables choppet up to youre liking
  • Once softened you can add in the rice, eggs and chicken
  • Season too you're liking and fry for a few minutes.
    •

    Add in the spring onions at the very end and now you are ready to plate up and enjoy!
